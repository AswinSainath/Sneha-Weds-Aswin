Day 1 – Nischayathartham

A sacred purification ritual where the bride and groom seek blessings for an obstacle-free wedding.

It is done to:
1. Seek blessings from God
2. Remove doshas (bad effects) or obstacles
3. Ensure a peaceful, happy married life for the couple

Formal engagement ceremony where both families exchange gifts, vows, and officially announce the alliance.

The word means:
Nischayam → decision / confirmation
Artham → declaration

So, it is the official announcement that the marriage is fixed.

A pre-wedding reception is a welcome celebration held before the wedding day.

It is organized to:
1. Introduce the bride and groom to guests
2. Welcome relatives coming from far places
3. Celebrate the upcoming marriage in a relaxed way
